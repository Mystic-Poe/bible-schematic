## Verse 28

> Do not fear, O Jacob My servant,” says the LORD, “For I <i>am</i> with you; For I will make a complete end of all the nations To which I have driven you, But I will not make a complete end of you. I will rightly correct you, For I will not leave you wholly unpunished.”
